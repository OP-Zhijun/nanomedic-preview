Inha University College of Medicine

Where spaceflight
becomes a laboratory
for aging.

Drug Delivery · Space Medicine · Smart Implantable Devices

We exploit the extreme conditions of microgravity and cosmic radiation to accelerate what takes decades on Earth — using space as a compressed-time model to engineer therapies for neurodegenerative disease, cancer, and age-related decline.
